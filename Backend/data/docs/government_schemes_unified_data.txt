[SCHEME_001]
SCHEME NAME: Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)
SOURCE PORTAL: https://agriwelfare.gov.in/ / https://www.myscheme.gov.in/
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Must be a landholding farmer family with cultivable land holding in their name.
- Both rural and urban landholder families are eligible.
- Institutional landholders, income tax payers, and high economic status individuals (e.g., constitutional post holders, retired pensioners drawing over INR 10,000/month, professionals like doctors/engineers) are strictly excluded.
LAST DATE TO APPLY: Open-ended rolling registration; corrections and fresh cycles refresh trimonthly matching installment releases.
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- Citizenship / Domicile Certificate
- Aadhaar Card (Mandatory for biometric/identity seeding)
- Landholding Papers / Land Revenue Records (Khata/Khesra numbers)
- Bank Account Passbook (Aadhaar-enabled bank account for DBT)
- Active Mobile Number for OTP validation

[SCHEME_002]
SCHEME NAME: AICTE Pragati Scholarship Scheme for Girl Students
SOURCE PORTAL: https://www.aicte-india.org/schemes/students-development-schemes / https://scholarships.gov.in/
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- The candidate must be a female student.
- Must be admitted to the 1st year of a Degree/Diploma level course or 2nd year of a Degree/Diploma course via lateral entry in any AICTE-approved institution.
- Maximum of two girl children per family are eligible.
- Total family income from all sources must not exceed INR 8,00,000 per annum.
LAST DATE TO APPLY: December 31st annually (Varies slightly based on extended academic admissions calendar).
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- Marksheet of 10th / 12th / ITI / Diploma equivalent examination
- Annual Income Certificate issued by an officer not below the rank of Tehsildar
- Admission letter issued by the centralized admission authority for the technical course
- Tuition fee receipt showing payment details
- Active Bank Passbook clearly displaying Account Holder Name, IFSC Code, and Photo (Aadhaar linked)
- Duly signed Declaration by the Parent/Guardian confirming the family info

[SCHEME_003]
SCHEME NAME: Ayushman Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY)
SOURCE PORTAL: https://nha.gov.in/ / https://www.myscheme.gov.in/
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Automatically determined based on SECC 2011 (Socio-Economic Caste Census) deprivation criteria for rural and urban occupational profiles.
- Covers households with no adult male member aged 16-59, kucha houses, landless households deriving major income from manual casual labor.
- In urban areas, specified occupational categories (e.g., ragpickers, beggars, domestic workers, street vendors, construction workers) qualify.
LAST DATE TO APPLY: Continuous enrollment; generation of Ayushman Cards runs round-the-year at CSCs and empaneled hospitals.
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- Aadhaar Card / Voter ID Card for individual identity mapping
- Ration Card / PM-JAY Letter / SECC Household ID Proof
- Active Mobile Number (for verification SMS)
- Domicile Certificate or valid residential ID

[SCHEME_004]
SCHEME NAME: PM Vidyalaxmi Scheme / Central Sector Interest Subsidy (CSIS)
SOURCE PORTAL: https://scholarships.gov.in/ / https://www.india.gov.in/
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Student must have secured admission into a professional/technical higher education course in India in an approved Institution.
- Must have taken an education loan under the Model Education Loan Scheme of IBA.
- Total parental/family annual income must be up to INR 4,500,000 per annum.
- Subsidy is applicable only once, either for undergraduate or postgraduate combined courses.
LAST DATE TO APPLY: Check individual annual cycles on the portal, usually closing by November 30th or December 31st.
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- Income Certificate from authorized state authorities
- Educational Loan Sanction Letter from the scheduled bank
- Bonafide Certificate from the enrolled Higher Educational Institution
- Course Fee structure outline
- Aadhaar Card and student PAN card

[SCHEME_005]
SCHEME NAME: Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)
SOURCE PORTAL: https://wcd.gov.in/ / https://www.myscheme.gov.in/
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Restricted to Pregnant Women and Lactating Mothers (PW&LM) for the first and second child (if the second child is a girl).
- The mother's age must not be less than 19 years at the time of pregnancy registration.
- Women in regular employment with the Central Government, State Governments, Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs), or those receiving similar structural benefits are excluded.
LAST DATE TO APPLY: Within 270 days from the date of childbirth or as per the specific institutional pregnancy tracking records.
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- Mother's Aadhaar Card (Mandatory)
- Husband’s Aadhaar Card
- MCP Card (Mother and Child Protection Card) showing registration and antenatal checkup details
- Bank Account Passbook of the beneficiary mapped explicitly to Aadhaar
- Birth Certificate of the infant (For late installment validation)

[SCHEME_006]
SCHEME NAME: Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY - Short Term Training)
SOURCE PORTAL: https://msde.gov.in/ / https://www.myscheme.gov.in/
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Any school/college dropout or unemployed youth looking for industry-relevant skill training.
- Age must be between 15 and 45 years.
- Must possess a verifiable identity card (preferably Aadhaar card) and an active bank account.
- Must meet the specific foundational educational requirements defined by the Sector Skill Council (SSC) for the chosen job role.
LAST DATE TO APPLY: Rolling registrations based on operational batches across authorized training centers.
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- Aadhaar Card (Mandatory for biometric tracking and attendance)
- Educational Qualification Marksheet/Certificate (e.g., 8th, 10th, or 12th pass out records)
- Bank Account Passbook copy
- Recent passport-size colored photographs
