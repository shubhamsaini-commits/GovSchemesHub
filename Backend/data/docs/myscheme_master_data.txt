[SCHEME_001]
SCHEME NAME: Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Age limit between 18 and 70 years.
- Must hold an active individual bank account linked with Aadhaar.
- Must provide explicit consent to enable the auto-debit facility for annual premium payment.
LAST DATE TO APPLY: June 1st of every calendar year (Auto-renewal cycle begins annually on June 1st; applications roll continuously).
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- Aadhaar Card (Primary Identity Proof)
- Bank Account Passbook / Statement
- Completed Application Cum Auto-Debit Consent Form
- Active Mobile Number (Linked to Bank Account)
- Nominee Details & Identification Documents

[SCHEME_002]
SCHEME NAME: Stand-Up India Scheme
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Must belong to the Scheduled Caste (SC), Scheduled Tribe (ST), or be a Woman Entrepreneur.
- Age must be 18 years or older.
- The financial assistance is available only for setting up Greenfield (First-time) projects/enterprises.
- The enterprise can be in the Manufacturing, Services, Trading, or Agri-allied sectors.
- In case of non-individual enterprises, at least 51% of the shareholding and controlling stake must be held by an SC/ST or Woman entrepreneur.
- The applicant must not be a defaulter to any bank or financial institution.
LAST DATE TO APPLY: December 31st of the current fiscal year (Open-ended rolling admissions subject to annual financial allocations).
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- Proof of Identity (Aadhaar Card / Voter ID / Passport / Driving License)
- Proof of Category (SC/ST Certificate issued by competent authority, if applicable)
- Business Enterprise Address Proof & Incorporation Documents
- Detailed Project Report (DPR) highlighting manufacturing/service details
- Partnership Deed / Memorandum and Articles of Association (for non-individual entities)
- Bank Account Statements for the last 6 months
- Passport size photographs of the promoters (2 copies)

[SCHEME_003]
SCHEME NAME: Prime Minister's Scholarship Scheme (PMSS)
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Applicants must be dependent wards or widows of Ex-Servicemen (ESM), Ex-Coast Guard personnel, or deceased personnel.
- Must be pursuing their first professional degree course (Engineering, Medical, Dental, Veterinary, B.Pharmacy, MBA, MCA, etc.) approved by regulatory bodies (AICTE, MCI, UGC).
- Must have secured a minimum of 60% marks in the Minimum Educational Qualification (MEQ) i.e., 10+2 / Diploma / Graduation.
- Students studying in their second or subsequent academic years are not eligible to apply as fresh candidates.
- Distance learning or vocational courses are explicitly excluded from this scholarship.
LAST DATE TO APPLY: December 31st annually (For fresh academic year registration batches).
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- Passport size photograph of the student
- Ex-Servicemen Certificate / Discharge Book (PPO containing dependent details)
- Certificate from the educational institution confirming admission under the respective course
- Mark sheet of 10+2 / Diploma / Graduation (MEQ verification)
- Bonafide Student Certificate signed by the Dean/Principal of the college
- Aadhaar Card linked to the student's individual bank account
- Bank Passbook first page showing Account Number and IFSC code clearly
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[SCHEME_004]
SCHEME NAME: Educational Assistance to 10th & 12th Students (MBOCWW - Maharashtra)
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- The parents or legal guardians of the student must be registered and active workers under the Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board.
- The student must have cleared the 10th or 12th standard examination in the immediate past academic year.
- Must have scored a minimum aggregate of 50% or more marks in the final board examinations.
- The benefits under this specific welfare scheme are capped at a maximum of two children per registered worker family.
LAST DATE TO APPLY: October 31st of the academic year following the declaration of board exam results.
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- Passport size photograph of the student applicant
- Student Aadhaar Card
- Identity Card of the parent issued by the Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board
- Valid and clear Board Mark sheet of 10th or 12th standard examination
- Active Bank Passbook of the student or joint account with the registered parent
- Proof of Residence inside the state of Maharashtra (Aadhaar/Ration Card/Electricity Bill)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[SCHEME_005]
SCHEME NAME: Old Age Samman Allowance (Haryana)
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- The applicant must be a senior citizen aged 60 years or older.
- Must be a permanent resident/domicile of the state of Haryana for the last 15 consecutive years.
- The combined annual income of the applicant and their spouse from all sources must not exceed INR 3,00,000 per annum.
- The applicant must not be receiving any other government pension, retirement benefits, or public sector allowance.
LAST DATE TO APPLY: Rolling Application / Open continuously throughout the calendar year.
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- Family ID (Parivar Pehchan Patra - PPP)
- Aadhaar Card
- Birth Certificate / School Leaving Certificate / Electoral Roll (Age Proof verification)
- Domicile Certificate of Haryana
- Income Certificate verified by local revenue authorities
- Joint Bank Account details with Aadhaar mapping

[SCHEME_006]
SCHEME NAME: Udyogini Scheme (Karnataka)
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Only women entrepreneurs are eligible to apply for this micro-enterprise scheme.
- Age of the applicant must fall within the range of 18 to 55 years.
- The family's total annual income must be below INR 1,500,000 for general categories (Income limit waived for SC/ST, disabled, or widowed applicants).
- The financial credit must be utilized for launching tiny/small retail, service, or micro-manufacturing businesses listed in the schedule.
LAST DATE TO APPLY: November 30th of the current financial allocation window.
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- Aadhaar Card / Voter Identification Card
- Caste or Category Certificate (for SC/ST or Special Category verification)
- Income Certificate issued by the Tahsildar
- BPL Card / Ration Card
- Detailed Project Estimate or Business Proposal Quote
- Two passport size color photographs
