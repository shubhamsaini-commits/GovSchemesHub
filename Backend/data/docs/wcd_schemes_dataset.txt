======================================================================
SCHEME DATA COMPILATION - MINISTRY OF WOMEN & CHILD DEVELOPMENT (WCD)
======================================================================

[SCHEME 1]
Scheme Name: Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)
Eligibility Criteria: Pregnant Women and Lactating Mothers (PW&LM) for their first and second child (if the second child is a girl). Excludes those in regular employment with Central/State Govts or PSUs.
Last Date to Apply: Within 270 days from the date of child birth.
Required Documents: Aadhaar Card (of beneficiary), Mapped Bank Passbook, Mother & Child Protection (MCP) Card, Birth Registration Certificate, Eligibility proof (e.g., e-Shram card, BPL card, or income certificate < 8 Lakhs).
Official Apply Website: https://pmmvy.wcd.gov.in/

[SCHEME 2]
Scheme Name: PM CARES for Children Scheme
Eligibility Criteria: Children who have lost both parents, legal guardians, adoptive parents, or a surviving parent due to the COVID-19 pandemic between 11th March 2020 and 5th May 2022.
Last Date to Apply: Registration window closed for historical cases; open for active ongoing verification/appeals.
Required Documents: Death Certificates of parents, COVID-19 positive report/medical proof of death, Child's Aadhaar Card, Guardianship documents.
Official Apply Website: https://pmcaresforchildren.in/

[SCHEME 3]
Scheme Name: Mission Shakti - Hub for Empowerment of Women (HEW)
Eligibility Criteria: Rural and urban women looking for single-window facilitation for inter-sectoral convergence, micro-financing, skill development, and legal aid support.
Last Date to Apply: Continuous / Ongoing enrollment via local district hubs.
Required Documents: Aadhaar card, Address proof, Income declaration certificate, Passport size photograph.
Official Apply Website: https://wcd.gov.in/

[SCHEME 4]
Scheme Name: Sakhi - One Stop Centre (OSC) Scheme
Eligibility Criteria: All women affected by violence (domestic, sexual, workplace harassment, or societal abuse) irrespective of age, marital status, caste, class, or religion.
Last Date to Apply: 24/7 emergency and immediate drop-in assistance (No deadline).
Required Documents: No immediate documents required for emergency rescue. For long-term legal aid/rehabilitation: Identity proof, copy of FIR/DIR (if filed).
Official Apply Website: https://wcd.gov.in/

[SCHEME 5]
Scheme Name: Mission Vatsalaya (Child Welfare and Sponsorship)
Eligibility Criteria: Children in difficult circumstances (orphans, abandoned, street children, victims of disasters/trafficking) whose family income is less than Rs. 72,000 per annum in urban areas or Rs. 60,000 in rural areas.
Last Date to Apply: Continuous enrollment via District Child Protection Units (DCPU).
Required Documents: Income certificate, Child's birth proof/Aadhaar card, Death certificates of parents (if applicable), DCPU recommendation report.
Official Apply Website: https://missionvatsalya.wcd.gov.in/

[SCHEME 6]
Scheme Name: Poshan Abhiyaan (National Nutrition Mission)
Eligibility Criteria: Targeted benefit framework supporting children (0-6 years), pregnant women, lactating mothers, and adolescent girls (14-18 years) in aspirational districts.
Last Date to Apply: Automated cyclic tracking through local Anganwadi Centers (Continuous).
Required Documents: Aadhaar Card of the mother/child, Anganwadi Registration Number, Health Tracker/Growth monitoring card.
Official Apply Website: https://poshanabhiyaan.gov.in/

[SCHEME 7]
Scheme Name: Working Women Hostel Scheme (Sakhi Niwas)
Eligibility Criteria: Working women (single, widowed, divorced, or married whose family income does not exceed Rs. 50,000 pm in metros or Rs. 40,000 pm elsewhere). Children up to 18 (girls) and 5 (boys) can stay with mothers.
Last Date to Apply: Subject to seat availability at respective hostels.
Required Documents: Employment certificate/Salary slip, Identity proof (Aadhaar/Passport), Permanent address proof, Medical fitness certificate.
Official Apply Website: https://wcd.gov.in/

[SCHEME 8]
Scheme Name: Scheme for Adolescent Girls (SAG)
Eligibility Criteria: Out-of-school adolescent girls in the age group of 14-18 years to provide nutritional support, life-skill education, and vocational training.
Last Date to Apply: Continuous registration at local Anganwadi tracks.
Required Documents: Aadhaar card, School dropout certificate/Self-declaration, Age proof document.
Official Apply Website: https://wcd.gov.in/

[SCHEME 9]
Scheme Name: Central Adoption Resource Authority (CARA) Registration
Eligibility Criteria: Prospective Adoptive Parents (PAPs) who are physically, mentally, and financially stable. Single females can adopt any gender; single males cannot adopt a girl child. Minimum stable marital status of 2 years required.
Last Date to Apply: Ongoing online registration queue.
Required Documents: Marriage certificate (if applicable), Income proof (ITR/Salary slip), Medical fitness certificate, Home study report by specialized adoption agency.
Official Apply Website: https://cara.wcd.gov.in/

[SCHEME 10]
Scheme Name: Nari Shakti Puraskar
Eligibility Criteria: Individual women (minimum age 25 years) and institutions/NGOs that have rendered exceptional service towards the cause of women empowerment.
Last Date to Apply: Announced annually (Usually between October and December).
Required Documents: Detailed resume/achievements report, Supporting media items/publications, Identity proofs, Incorporation certificate (for NGOs).
Official Apply Website: https://awards.gov.in/
