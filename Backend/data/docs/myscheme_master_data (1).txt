[SCHEME_001]
SCHEME NAME: Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Age limit between 18 and 70 years.
- Must hold an active individual bank account linked with Aadhaar.
- Must provide explicit consent to enable the auto-debit facility for annual premium payment.
LAST DATE TO APPLY: June 1st of every calendar year (Auto-renewal cycle begins annually on June 1st; applications roll continuously).
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- Aadhaar Card (Primary Identity Proof)
- Bank Account Passbook / Statement
- Completed Application Cum Auto-Debit Consent Form
- Active Mobile Number (Linked to Bank Account)
- Nominee Details & Identification Documents

[SCHEME_002]
SCHEME NAME: Stand-Up India Scheme
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Must belong to the Scheduled Caste (SC), Scheduled Tribe (ST), or be a Woman Entrepreneur.
- Age must be 18 years or older.
- The financial assistance is available only for setting up Greenfield (First-time) projects/enterprises.
- The enterprise can be in the Manufacturing, Services, Trading, or Agri-allied sectors.
- In case of non-individual enterprises, at least 51% of the shareholding and controlling stake must be held by an SC/ST or Woman entrepreneur.
- The applicant must not be a defaulter to any bank or financial institution.
LAST DATE TO APPLY: December 31st of the current fiscal year (Open-ended rolling admissions subject to annual financial allocations).
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- Proof of Identity (Aadhaar Card / Voter ID / Passport / Driving License)
- Proof of Category (SC/ST Certificate issued by competent authority, if applicable)
- Business Enterprise Address Proof & Incorporation Documents
- Detailed Project Report (DPR) highlighting manufacturing/service details
- Partnership Deed / Memorandum and Articles of Association (for non-individual entities)
- Bank Account Statements for the last 6 months
- Passport size photographs of the promoters (2 copies)

[SCHEME_003]
SCHEME NAME: Pradhan Mantri Uchchatar Shiksha Protsahan (PM-USP) - Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- The applicant must be pursuing regular courses at colleges/ institutions recognized by AICTE or respective Regulatory Bodies.
- Gross parental/ family income of the applicant should not exceed INR 4,50,000/- per annum.
- Students pursuing correspondence, distance mode, or pursuing Diploma courses are not eligible.
- Students who are already availing the benefit from any other scholarship schemes are excluded.
LAST DATE TO APPLY: December 31st annually (Varies slightly matching extended academic timelines).
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- Marksheet of 10th / 12th standard examination
- Income Certificate issued by an authorized state revenue authority
- Aadhaar Card (Mandatory for Aadhaar e-KYC)
- Active Bank Passbook front page copy showing Account Number and IFSC code clearly
- Bonafide Certificate from the educational institution (if studying out of the domicile state)

[SCHEME_004]
SCHEME NAME: Special Incentive to OBC Meritorious Students
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Open to OBC (Other Backward Class) students who have scored 80% or above in their HSSLC examination.
- The annual income of the applicant's parents must not exceed INR 5,00,000/-.
- The scheme recognizes HSSLC results from verified education boards (SEBA, CBSE, ICSE).
- A valid OBC certificate (mentioning sub-caste) issued by the competent authority is mandatory.
LAST DATE TO APPLY: August 31st of the active academic cycle.
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- Caste Certificate (valid OBC certificate, inserting sub-caste)
- Parents' Income Certificate, issued by the Circle Officer
- HSSLC Admit Card and Marksheet
- HSLC Marksheet
- Front Page of Bank Passbook
- Aadhaar Card / PAN Card

[SCHEME_005]
SCHEME NAME: Old Age Samman Allowance (Haryana)
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- The applicant must be a senior citizen aged 60 years or older.
- Must be a permanent resident/domicile of the state of Haryana.
- The combined annual income of the applicant and their spouse from all sources must not exceed INR 3,00,000 per annum.
- The applicant must not be receiving any other government pension or public sector allowance.
LAST DATE TO APPLY: Rolling Application / Open continuously throughout the calendar year.
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- Family ID (Parivar Pehchan Patra - PPP)
- Aadhaar Card
- Birth Certificate / School Leaving Certificate (Age Proof verification)
- Domicile Certificate of Haryana
- Income Certificate verified by local revenue authorities
- Joint Bank Account details with Aadhaar mapping

[SCHEME_006]
SCHEME NAME: Udyogini Scheme (Karnataka)
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Only women entrepreneurs are eligible to apply for this micro-enterprise scheme.
- Age of the applicant must fall within the range of 18 to 55 years.
- The family's total annual income must be below INR 1,50,000 for general categories (Income limit waived for SC/ST, disabled, or widowed applicants).
- The financial credit must be utilized for launching tiny/small retail, service, or micro-manufacturing businesses.
LAST DATE TO APPLY: November 30th of the current financial allocation window.
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- Aadhaar Card / Voter Identification Card
- Caste or Category Certificate (for SC/ST or Special Category verification)
- Income Certificate issued by the Tahsildar
- BPL Card / Ration Card
- Detailed Project Estimate or Business Proposal Quote
- Two passport size color photographs
